Togetherlist kicked off on 5pm on Wednesday, 11/9. In 3 days, a team of contributors banded together to create a comprehensive searchable database of organizations focused on supporting our communities. We self organized across timezones and through a dedicated team effort, we launched Saturday, November 12th at 8PM.

Togetherlist collaborated on this risepad across multiple timezones and various disciplines. We were inspired by occupy sandy, Jezebel list, nomadlist, votegif. We are using google forms, neocities, googlefonts and namecheap.

Thank you to our contributors for making this possible, the organizations for the tireless work you do for our communities.

And every person who has supported or amplified our voices through this project and countless other ways.

Because we are stronger together.

- the togetherlist team
